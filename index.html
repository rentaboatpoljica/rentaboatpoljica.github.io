GOOD TO KNOW

Frequently asked questions

Is fuel included?

Fuel for the more powerful boats is charged according to consumption. Fuel is included for the Vodice 480.

How much is the deposit?

The deposit depends on the boat and is usually between €100 and €300. It is returned after the boat is brought back in good condition.

What are the rental hours?

A full-day rental runs from 09:00 to 18:00. Half-day rentals are available from 09:00 to 14:00 or from 13:00 to 18:00.

Are children and pets allowed on board?

Yes, children and pets are welcome on board. Children must be supervised by an adult, and please let us know in advance if you are bringing a pet.
